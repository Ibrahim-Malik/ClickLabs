Biological Air Sampler BAS-111

Description :

The Biological Air Sampler BAS 111 is equipped with a microprocessor-controlled digital display and temperature adjustment in 0.1°C increments. For easy monitoring, it features a large digital display. Designed with a state-of-the-art forced cooling system, it ensures the interior temperature remains stable and uniform. It also includes a comprehensive temperature alarm system, with a temperature variation of less than 3°C in an ambient range of 10 to 32°C. Features
  • The Biological Air Sampler is a highly efficient laboratory instrument designed for accurate collection of airborne particles, including bacteria, fungi, and other microorganisms. It utilizes a precise suction mechanism to draw air through a collection chamber, where particles are trapped on an agar plate or filter for later analysis. This sampler is equipped with a microprocessor-controlled digital display that allows for easy monitoring and adjustment of settings. The temperature control system ensures stable and consistent conditions, with temperature adjustments in 0.1°C increments, essential for preserving sample integrity. The forced-air cooling system provides optimized airflow, maintaining a uniform temperature inside the sampler. This helps in ensuring consistent sampling conditions, preventing temperature fluctuations that could affect results. Additionally, the unit features a comprehensive temperature alarm system that alerts users to any deviations beyond the set range. For user convenience, the Biological Air Sampler comes with a large digital display screen, allowing for clear visibility of operating parameters. A lock system is included to prevent unauthorized access, ensuring the security of sensitive samples. The adjustable shelves and inner LED light facilitate easy sample collection and handling, even in low-light conditions.
